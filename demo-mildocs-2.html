LEGAL DOCUMENTS

WILL, POWERS OF ATTORNEY, END-OF-LIFE CARE & DIRECTIVES

As a service member, free legal assistance is available to you at your installation. A Last Will and Testament is a good place to start. Powers of Attorney may be used to appoint an agent when a service member will not physically be present to take care of financial matters. You should keep current copies of all these document in a safe place. Medical directives, funeral plans, and end-of-life plans can be stored here as well.

YOU SHOULD ALSO KNOW...

The legal documents in your possession will change over time. Items like you Last Will & Testament need to be updated as your family changes. Check out the online resources for more information and visit your installation legal office.

Hint: Your local military treatment facility can keep your medical directive on file for you.
